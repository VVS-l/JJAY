Beat Licensing

All beats available on this website are subject to licensing agreements. Purchasing a license grants you certain rights to use the beat, depending on the terms of the specific license.

Rights & Restrictions

- Free use of beats requires proper credit in the title or description (e.g., "Prod. JJAY").
- Using beats on streaming platforms without a license will result in a takedown request.
- Continued unauthorized use will lead to copyright infringement lawsuits.

Beat Licensing Agreement

Before purchasing a beat, you are required to review and sign the Beat Licensing Agreement. This agreement outlines your rights and responsibilities when using the beats, ensuring a fair and professional transaction.
